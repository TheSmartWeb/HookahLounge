Our Story

Elevating Nightlife Since 2015

Cloud9 Hookah Lounge was born from a passion for creating unforgettable nightlife experiences. Our founders, avid travelers and flavor enthusiasts, wanted to bring the authentic hookah culture to the heart of the city.

We source only the highest quality tobacco and ingredients from around the world, ensuring each session is a journey for your senses. Our expert mixologists craft signature cocktails that perfectly complement our shisha flavors.

More than just a lounge, Cloud9 is a community where music, flavors, and good company come together to create magical evenings.
